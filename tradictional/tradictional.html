Traditional Building Costs Calculator

You have heard us say that concrete printing is 70% faster and 1/3rd the cost, but compared to what?

We can make outlandish claims, but until you understand how to compare the cost savings of concrete printing to traditional practices, you will never make sense of it all. This is the primary reason we cant meet with customers that are unable to provide the information below from the actual past project. People get excited, people want to know more, but in the end it's meaningless unless they have the knowledge and numbers to make accurate comparisons.

Complete the worksheet below and you will have a better idea of where you will save. You will see the material and labor savings, but don't forget the other savings that aren't listed such as overall construction time, interest on construction loans, management to coordinate all subs, construction delays due to Sub failures to start and finish when they should. One sub failure can result in weeks of project delays. These are real costs that effect the profitability of every job. Only you know the true saving.
